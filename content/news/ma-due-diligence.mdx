## Beyond the Balance Sheet

M&A due diligence has traditionally focused on financial statements, legal exposure, and operational health. That framework is no longer sufficient.

Acquirers who succeed in today's market are those who can rapidly assess the quality of digital assets, data infrastructure, and ESG performance — elements that are increasingly determinative of long-term value creation.

## What's Changing

**ESG integration.** Regulatory pressure and investor expectations have made ESG due diligence a non-negotiable component of any significant transaction.

**Technology audit.** A target's technology stack, data governance practices, and cyber posture now carry material valuation implications.

**Talent assessment.** Particularly in knowledge-intensive industries, the quality and retention risk of key personnel has become a primary value driver.
